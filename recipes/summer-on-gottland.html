Gottländsk Sommar

Description

Swedish for summer on gottland, this spin on a gin sour takes inspiration from the beautiful baltic island, adding tastes of a rhubarb, elderflower and raspberries to make for a real showstopper of a drink.

1 Cocktail

You need:

  • Shaker
  • Cocktail Strainer
  • Cream Siphon

Ingredients:

  • 5cl pink gin
  • 2cl rose vermouth
  • 3cl lemon juice, preferably freshly pressed
  • 3dl sugar
  • 150 g raspberries
  • 1.5 dl water
  • 2dl rhubarb juice(Cordial)
  • 2dl elderflower juice(Cordial)
  • 3 egg whites

Steps:

  1. First we need to make the raspberry syrup, cook sugar, water and raspberry together until it starts to boil and the raspberries dissolve. Strain into a bottle and let it cool down.
  2. Then we make the rhubarb and elderflower foam, mix together the ingredients into your cream siphon, add two chargers and shake the siphon vigorously for 10-20 seconds.
  3. Now we can finally mix the drink, add ice, pink gin, rose vermouth, lemon juice and 2cl of your raspberry syrup to your shaker. Shake vigorously for 5-10 seconds until pour into a low glass with a few large ice cubes in it.
  4. Top the drink up with the foam from your cream siphon and serve, yum!
